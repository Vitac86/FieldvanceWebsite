# Fieldvance Landing Page — AI Context

## 1. Project Goal
Build a simple, credible, high-conviction one-page marketing website for Fieldvance.

The website should:
- explain the product in under 5 seconds
- show how it works
- present pricing clearly
- support English, Spanish, and Russian
- feel modern, clean, and enterprise-ready
- stay lightweight and easy to maintain

## 2. Product Summary
Fieldvance is a vertical, mobile-first AI workflow platform for HVAC and plumbing service companies.

It integrates with the customer’s CRM/FSM and turns each work order into a guided, validated technician workflow. It helps technicians arrive with better context, troubleshoot using approved technical knowledge, capture structured field evidence, generate a service report, and sync results back to the CRM/FSM.

## 3. Core Capabilities
- Pre-dispatch AI brief
- Guided mobile technician workflow
- RAG-based troubleshooting grounded in approved manuals, SOPs, and checklists
- Structured capture of actions, measurements, photos, parts used, and short voice notes
- Validation engine for required evidence and consistency checks
- Auto-generated service report
- Two-way CRM/FSM synchronization
- Supervisor web console

## 4. Audience
Primary audience:
- HVAC service companies
- Plumbing service companies
- Operations managers
- Service managers
- Founders / owners of field service businesses
- Supervisors responsible for quality, reporting, and technician performance

## 5. Core Positioning
Fieldvance is an AI workflow layer that augments existing CRM/FSM systems instead of replacing them.

It solves the last-mile field execution problem:
- technicians arrive with incomplete context
- diagnosis is improvised on site
- documentation is reconstructed later from scattered notes
- reports are slow, inconsistent, and error-prone

Fieldvance standardizes preparation, execution, evidence capture, and reporting in one workflow.

## 6. Key Proof Points
- Pilots with 3 service companies
- 18 daily active technicians
- Around 200 tracked service calls
- Average report time reduced from ~12 minutes to ~2 minutes
- Critical reporting errors reduced from ~22% to ~9%
